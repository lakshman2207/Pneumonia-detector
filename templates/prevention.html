Pneumonia Prevention Tips

Top Ways to Stay Protected

  • 💉 Get vaccinated against pneumococcal diseases and influenza
  • 🧼 Wash your hands regularly and use hand sanitizer
  • 🚭 Avoid smoking and second-hand smoke exposure
  • 🥦 Maintain a healthy diet and hydrate well
  • 🏃‍♀ Exercise regularly to build lung strength
  • 😷 Use masks in crowded or polluted areas

If You Suspect Pneumonia

If you experience persistent fever, chest pain, cough, or shortness of breath, consult a healthcare professional immediately. Don't self-medicate. RadiantAI can support your diagnosis—but it’s not a replacement for a doctor.

🧠 Did You Know? Pneumonia is the single largest infectious cause of death in children worldwide, according to the WHO.
